laravel

Laravel is a PHP open-source framework that allows us to build scalable web applications rapidly, smoothly, and with an elegant syntax. It provides great documentation, libraries, learning resources and an amazing community. It's an excellent fit for projects with a real agile focus and high-quality code standards.

Top Laravel Developers

According to Clutch, the B2B market analysis leader, we’re the number one Laravel Development company in Uruguay and we’re in the Top 30 worldwide rank. Our collaborators' Laravel skills are perfected when entering the team with a three-month intensive Laravel course created by us, and we have Laravel Ninja Mentors available within the team.

Laravel Community

We’re active leaders in the Laravel community! We have hosted numerous Laravel Meetups, published Laravel guides and articles, developed Laravel courses, and shared valuable packages. We love attending Laracon every year and we’re official Laravel News Partners.

Laravel Meetups

Light-it sponsors Laravel Meetups around the world and our teammates have been speakers in Laravel conferences in the US, Coephagen, Uruguay and Argentina. We love sharing these moments with the community!
